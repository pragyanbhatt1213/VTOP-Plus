The Problem

Why VTOP Plus?

The official VTOP portal gets the job done — but barely. Here's what students deal with every day.

Slow Portal

The official VTOP portal requires navigating multiple pages to find simple information, with frequent load delays.

Too Much Navigation

Students must open multiple nested menus just to check basic attendance or upcoming exam schedules.

Hard to Track Exams

Seat numbers, reporting times and venue details are buried deep — found right when you need them most.
